Butter chicken

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Yogurt
  • Butter
  • Tomatoes
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Red chili powder
  • Garam masala
  • Fresh cream
  • Salt

Procedure

  1. Marinate the chicken with yogurt and spices.
  2. Cook the chicken until tender.
  3. Prepare a tomato-based gravy with butter and spices.
  4. Add the chicken to the gravy and simmer.
  5. Stir in fresh cream and serve hot.
