Besan chilla

Ingredients

  • Besan (gram flour)
  • Onion
  • Green chili
  • Coriander leaves
  • Salt
  • Turmeric
  • Water
  • Oil

Procedure

  1. Mix all ingredients with water to make a smooth batter.
  2. Pour onto a hot pan and spread evenly.
  3. Cook until golden on both sides.
  4. Serve hot with chutney.
