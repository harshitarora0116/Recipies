Amritsari Kulcha

Ingredients required

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 2 tbsp yogurt
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp sugar
  • Water as needed
  • 2 boiled potatoes, mashed
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • 1 tbsp coriander leaves, chopped
  • ½ tsp garam masala
  • ½ tsp crushed anardana (dried pomegranate seeds)
  • Salt to taste
  • Kalonji (nigella seeds) for topping
  • Butter for serving

Procedure

  1. Mix flour, yogurt, oil, salt, sugar, and water to make a soft dough. Let it rest for 1–2 hours.
  2. Combine mashed potatoes, green chili, coriander, garam masala, anardana, and salt to prepare the stuffing.
  3. Divide the dough into portions, fill each with the potato mixture, and roll gently into flat rounds.
  4. Sprinkle kalonji on top and press lightly.
  5. Cook on a hot tawa until golden brown on both sides.
  6. Brush with butter and serve hot.
